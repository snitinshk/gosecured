Encryption and Privacy

Our “Zero Knowledge” approach means that even we can not access your data, as we utilize Off the Record (OTR) encrypted chat and ZRTP Encryption for voice and video. A direct end-to-end encrypted channel is created between you and the other party.

Offshore Protection

GoSecured is 100% offshore owned and operated, meaning we are outside the jurisdiction of any other country and especially the "Five Eyes" bound by the multilateral treaty for joint cooperation in signals intelligence. Our encrypted servers and drives are in the Republic of Panama, a country with some of the best privacy laws and data security in the world.

Talk to Your Existing Contacts

GoogleTalk, Facebook, Yahoo! Messenger, AIM, ICQ, MSN; GoSecured VC was created to make the move to secure communication easy and fast. Another big difference between GoSecured VC and programs like Skype is that we are built to enable you to keep existing accounts from different service providers. GoSecured VC supports many of the most popular and widely used IM and telephony protocols, like SIP, Jabber/XMPP (used by Facebook and Google Talk), AIM, ICQ, MSN, and Yahoo! Messenger to allow you to still communicate with your existing contacts. GoSecured VC also runs on all platforms including Windows, Mac OS, Linux, and Android.

Free Encrypted Voice and Video Calls

Make encrypted crystal-clear free voice calls or video calls all over the world to GoSecured VC or any other xmpp/japper user. Alternatively make secure low-cost phone calls to any U.S. and international cell phones and landlines via your EncryptVoice account. Don't let distance get in the way. Whether you're down the hall or across the world, securely and privately connect with your employees, partners, clients, or friends and family no matter where they happen to be.

Video Conferencing

Experience secure collaboration with simple to use, high-quality encrypted video conferencing and meeting tools. Start a private meeting, share easily and work together effectively. Show others anything on your computer via encrypted connection—files, apps, or your whole desktop. GoSecured VC allows you to collaborate face-to-face, maintaining a human connection with your employees, partners, clients, or friends and family no matter where they happen to be.

Screen Sharing

Share your computer's entire desktop, or anything on it. Use Screen Share to add files or show a image you want to talk about and enhance the conversation. With screen sharing, you can get everyone on the same page quickly and easily by adding a graph, chart, or image to your encrypted video chat. Use desktop sharing when words alone aren't enough to get your point across.

Encrypted Instant Messaging

GoSecured VC lets you have end-to-end encrypted and authenticated chat sessions with people so that only you and the person you’re chatting with can read the chat messages, and all other parties—our own servers, your ISP, or anyone else trying eavesdropping on your private and secure chats—cannot.

GoSecured VC encrypted instant text messages is the most secure way to communicate. The written word isn't dead; it's gone encrypted and digital. OTR chat keeps no record of the messages you send. Send encrypted text and instant messages to your contacts, even while you're video chatting.

Send Files

With GoSecured VC video chat, you can send files securely, quickly, and easily. Send secure files of any size over encrypted connection to single or multiple contacts during a voice, video, or group call and via instant message.
