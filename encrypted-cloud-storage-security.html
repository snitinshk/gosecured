Security

GoSecured® has set a new security standard with multilevel EncryptSync encryption. Complete your team’s projects with the confidence that each file you share, manage, and access is secure. Your data is always encrypted, offshore, and private.

Client-side encryption - True Privacy

EncryptSync ensures that you are always protected by encrypting data before the files leave your computer and providing end-to-end encryption. That is true privacy for you and your company. Unlike many other cloud providers, files shared with EncryptSync are encrypted both data-at-rest and data-in-flight.

We even provide the added security of an encryption password that will never be stored in any database or the server. What does this mean for you? Even with physical access to the storage servers, no one can decrypt your data, not even the EncryptSync staff.

Encryption Specifications

Our encryption technology includes 448-bit Blowfish, an encryption algorithm symmetric-key block cipher, which has one of the longest running reputations of zero effective cryptanalysis found to date. We chose this technology because it makes brute force key-exhaustion attacks nearly impossible by the initial key setup process.

EncryptSync also uses SSL and RSA-4096 protocols for client-server data transfers, which is completed after client-side encryption. Data encryption passwords are never stored in any database or the server.

Offshore Protection

Owned and operated in The Republic of Panama, GoSecured® is a 100% offshore company and outside the jurisdiction of any other country, including the “Five Eyes”, which are bound by the multilateral treaty for joint cooperation in signals intelligence.

The Republic of Panama, where our encrypted servers and drives are housed in an enterprise-class data center, has some of the best privacy laws and data security in the world—and you can take advantage of that peace of mind no matter where you are.
