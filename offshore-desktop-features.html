Anonymous Panama IP

Browse the internet privately, plus send and receive e-mail all from your Panama offshore desktop. The IP shows it originates in the Republic of Panama. No data is stored on your laptop, iPad, PC, etc. All communications and confidential files are kept private and secure under the Panama privacy protection laws, and safe from foreign governments, local ISP and other individuals.

Anywhere, Any Device

All you need is an internet connection, and users can securely access hosted virtual offshore desktops. Use any device in any location, including computers, iPads, smartphones and internet kiosks.

Windows Familiarity

Use your Offshore Cloud Desktop in a comfortable environment, as it resembles your desktop PC. Your offshore PC includes familiar programs, like Microsoft Office. You can also install any software an individual or business needs to your hosted offshore desktop within the familiar Windows environment.

Backups and Disaster Recovery

All of your data is automatically backed up several times, with our daily backups along with our weekly server snapshots. This will ensure that you never have to worry about losing valuable data. Protecting your data from a disaster is critical, and we have dedicated team members who specialize in data backup and disaster recovery procedures, ensuring your business continuity in the event of a disaster. We also deliver reduced latency while increasing aggregate bandwidth capacity.

Always Secure, Always Available

While your Windows hosted desktop is in the secure cloud and your team, regardless of geographic location, can transfer files safe and secure to your shared drive within your secure virtual network, you still have access to saving local, printing local, and continued utilization of other local devices.

Thin Client Option

Replace desktop computers with thin clients. These look, feel, and behave like normal computers, but don't have any computing power, storage, or memory of their own. No individual computers to manage, no software packages to add or remove, and everything (files, emails, web browsing, accounting) is held centrally, securely and privately in our offshore Panama Data Center.
