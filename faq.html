  • How difficult is it to transfer my service (email provider, domain, etc.) over to the service that Secured Nexus™ provides? Is there going to be any down time?

    Encryption
  • What is Encryption?
    • Encryption is the conversion of data into a form, called a ciphertext, which cannot be easily understood by unauthorized people. You must have the ability to “decode” or Decrypt the information using a highly technical process of converting the data back into its regular form so it can be understood.
  • I understand encryption, how does yours work?
    • We encrypt and decrypt your incoming and outgoing email at the gateway level, meaning before it leaves the server and as soon as it comes back to the server. We use S/MIME and PDF Encryption. S/MIME provides authentication, message integrity and non-repudiation (using X.509 certificates) and protection against message interception. S/MIME uses public key encryption (PKI) for encryption and signing. All of this is done automatically for you.
  • How do you secure my PDF documents?
    • We use a 20 byte one-time password encryption key which can be changed per user. Your receiver can generate his one-time password (OTP) by our 4096-Bit encrypted portal. The receiver can then reply directly from the encrypted PDF for continued end-to-end encryption.
