Encrypted Connection

A 128-bit encryption algorithm connects to your remote PC. In cryptography, RC4 (also known as ARC4 or ARCFOUR) is the most widely used software stream cipher and is used in popular protocols such as Transport Layer Security (TLS) to protect Internet traffic.

Enterprise-Class Republic of Panama Data Center

Your private data is protected by our enterprise-class Panama Data Center, accredited and certified to ISO27001 and PCI DSS security standards. All transmissions to and from your offshore hosted desktop are encrypted, and utilize our enterprise-grade firewalls with antivirus software and intrusion detection to monitor and block any unauthorized access.

Security-First Architecture

GoSecured uses our Enhanced Secure architecture which supports enterprise applications. The architecture scales as needed, and meets the performance, availability, automation, and security service-level requirements.
