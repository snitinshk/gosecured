With GoSecured Call, you get automatically encrypted calling to anyone who uses ZRTP. We believe you have the right to communicate privately and securely.

Encrypted Calling

GoSecured Call’s “Zero Knowledge” approach means even we cannot access your data or listen to your calls. You get a direct end-to-end encrypted channel between you and the other party.

Private GoSecured Call Number

Get a dedicated, unique 5-digit number for internal use with each device you register for GoSecured Call. Securely dial any GoSecured Call number with the knowledge that your call never leaves our offshore servers.

Offshore Location

What does our 100% offshore owned and operated location mean for you? We are outside any other country’s jurisdiction, so we are not bound by the multilateral treaty for joint cooperation in signals intelligence by which the “Five Eyes” countries must abide. Our encrypted servers and drives operate out of the Republic of Panama, which boasts some of the best privacy laws and data security in the world.

International and Toll Free Numbers

Build your presence in optimal areas with toll-free numbers and a wide selection of local numbers available in over 60 countries. With 400 international city prefixes and over 7,000 prefixes across the USA and Canada, you have a large selection of numbers available for your customers to contact you. We also make it easy to keep your existing customers happy by transferring your existing phone numbers.

Works Across All Your Locations

Since your phone system is not bound to one location, you and your team gain the mobility and freedom to communicate via smartphone or tablet anywhere, anytime. GoSecured Call is highly adaptable and customizable, making it perfect for managing multiple office locations or remote employees.

Desktop Phone, PC, or Mobile

With an Internet connection you can use GoSecured Call with IP deskphone, PC, smartphone, or tablet. The GoSecured Call App even turns mobile BYOD devices into encrypted business phones. Manage your entire system from any location.

Virtual Receptionist

GoSecured Call features an Auto-Receptionist that takes care of answering rules, such as greetings, hours of operations, and voicemail. This allows your employees to take the important calls and stay productive on high-priority tasks. You can easily create multiple departments (i.e. sales, billing, support), each with its own unique greeting and call routing configurations.

Conference Rooms

Our Conferencing Bridge is compatible with any phone system and provides conference calling for up to 500 participants—all delivered in High Definition Voice quality. Participants can call in by direct number, extension, or virtual receptionist. We even provide the option for a password protected conference room.

Custom Greetings & Hold Music

You are in control of customized music selections or professionally recorded greetings to play while calls are on hold.

Extension Transfer

Contacting someone in your internal network is quick and easy, even when transferring a call.

Hours of Operation

Easily configure the date and time to control when your phones ring your office, forward, or go to voicemail.

Virtual Fax

Fax machines are outdated. Throw out the old fax that only takes up space in your office. You can send and receive faxes electronically with GoSecured Call.
