One-Click Encryption

Easily send encrypted email from the secure webmail or from any email client like Thunderbird, Outlook, and more with IMAP/POP. The GoSecured Mail system will intelligently decide the most secure encryption method to use. Alternatively, rules can be placed at the user or domain level to automatically encrypt email for a no-click encrypted email solution. Encrypt email and all attachments with S/MIME PKI (X.509, RFC 3280) or PDF AES 128 bit encryption all with the easy-to-use and nothing-to-install GoSecured Mail System.

Send To Anyone, Using Anything

With GoSecured Mail the receiver can decrypt the encrypted email on almost any platform or email service, such as PC, Mac, Linux, Android, iPhone, iPad, Blackberry, and Windows Mobile phones. The receiver can also decrypt the encrypted email on all webmail like Gmail, Hotmail, Outlook OWA or any system with an internet connection and an internet browser.

No Additional Software

Send encrypted to anyone regardless their mail service, encryption, or even if they don't use encryption. It is easy to receive and read an GoSecured Mail encrypted email, whether you are part of the same organization, external customer or partner. When both parties are on the GoSecured Mail platform Encrypted Messages never leave the server.

If the Recipient uses S/MIME the GoSecured Mail will automatically do the key exchange and take care of the rest. If the receiver is on an outside system like Gmail and doesn't use S/MIME, then an Encrypted PDF is sent with an easy to follow walkthrough email on generating the secure OTP (one-time password) to unlock the secure PDF, from which they can reply encrypted, directly from the Encrypted PDF.

Encrypted Between Customers and Clients

With GoSecured Mail the receiver can open, read, and even reply encrypted to the encrypted email. Companies can establish an easy to use Encrypted Connection with customers and partners. The recipient will register generating their own password to open encrypted emails sent via Encrypted PDF in the future. Ensuring only the intended recipient can access confidential information.

Fully Compliant

Secure S/MIME PKI (X.509, RFC 3280) and PDF AES 128 bit encryption complies with PCI/DSS, HIPAA Hitech, GLBA, Sarbanes Oxley Act (SOX), FTC Red Flag Rules and numerous others. You can set your own security policies that the system will are automatically enforce for all users.

Automatic Authentication

GoSecured Mail creates a secure end-to-end virtual channel between the sender and receiver. The first time a secured email is sent the receiver needs to authenticate by registering and creating an GoSecured Mail password to generate an OTP (one-time password).

Key management has historically been an issue with encryption solutions, with the difficulty of users knowing how to exchange keys and import/export to each email client used. GoSecured Mail is a fully automated solution. 4096 Bit RSA Encryption certificates are automatically generated and synchronized between server and client, so there is no need for manual key management.
