Keep files Encrypted and Protected

We put your security first. EncryptSync features end-to-end encrypted, secure, and simple file management. Your data is encrypted before leaving your computer, and the password used to encrypt the data is not stored in any database or the server, ensuring added security. The reliable team at our offshore data center ensures that we are not prey to third party pressures often associated with cloud providers. EncryptSync brings you all the benefits of the cloud without the security risks.

Secure Backup & Revisions

Never again lose a file, folder, or previous versions of a file or folder. EncryptSync will securely store and back up your files online, so you maintain access no matter where you are. Your encrypted files and the previous versions are under your control in case you need access to multiple versions of files or a file was accidentally deleted. This convenient feature lets you restore files and control the number of versions that are stored, so there is no need to stress or lose time redoing work that was not backed up.

Sync Securely

With EncryptSync, you can keep your files in sync automatically and securely, so each member of your team has the most up-to-date version even when accessing file from online and across multiple computers. You can also be confident that your data is synced across your phones, laptops, and tablets. Wherever you are, you're viewing current data. Files added to your EncryptSync folder will automatically appear on all of your devices. When you make changes at one computer or device, they instantly appear on all others, including your home computer, work computer, smart phone, and tablet.

Share Privately

Regardless of file size, you can share with co-workers, partners, clients, or friends & family without worry of outside parties viewing your information. EncryptSync allows easy file sharing and the ability to send a direct link to a file to anyone, whether they are in the EncryptSync System or not. And, you retain the ability to make changes to files even after sending a link – all of your updates will be synced. With our encryption system, you are in control, and can remove the file link at any time.

Secure collaboration

You set the permissions so that access to files and folders stays in the hands of selected team members and co-workers. Designed with businesses in mind, EncryptSync provides secure file storage in one central and encrypted location for companies, associations, communities, or teams. With EncryptSync Groups, you and your team can share a storage amount and collaborate and sync folders as a team. The system allows for ease of collaboration, so you get the job done efficiently by simply setting the proper permissions for each contact, such as co-owner; view & sync; view, sync, & delete; or sync only. Work together to edit documents, or leave comments for each other securely.
